Strategic Recommendations
Key actions for the CRO
1. Stem the Bleeding
Net logos down 291 since FY24. FY25 churned ARR spiked 4x to $25.3M. Focus retention playbooks on accounts 1–5 years into tenure.
2. Protect the Long Tail
Accounts <$10K churn at 12–33%. Move sub-$25K accounts up-band through cross-sell or accept managed attrition with automated engagement.
3. Drive Multi-Product & SaaS
80% of churned held only 1 product vs 56% of active. Non-SaaS customers churn at far higher rates. SaaS conversion is a retention lever.
4. Winback Account Potential
4,016 active churned companies at 6% AP vs 46% for current customers. Prioritize recently churned mid-to-large accounts ($25K+ last ARR).
